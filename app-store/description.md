FocusLock helps you do your best work by locking out distractions and keeping you in flow states longer.

**Deep Work, Simplified**
Set a focus session. Lock your screen from notifications. Get things done. FocusLock gets out of your way so you can do your best thinking.

Whether you're writing code, studying for exams, or grinding a deadline — FocusLock gives you the structure to stay in the zone without willpower games.

• Focus timer with customizable session lengths (25 min, 50 min, 90 min, custom)

• Session history — see your focus streaks and total deep work hours

• Widget support — start sessions from your home screen

FocusLock is built around the principles of deep work and timeboxing — techniques used by elite performers to produce high-quality output in less time.
